# DouXia — Own Your AI Conversations

![DouXia Logo](/assets/resources/douxia/logo_256.png)

**The desktop app that turns your scattered AI chats into organized, searchable, exportable knowledge.**

## Problem

You ask AI assistants dozens of questions every day — coding problems, research deep-dives, writing drafts, life advice. Over months, you build up hundreds of valuable conversations across Doubao, Yuanbao, DeepSeek, ChatGPT, and others. But all that knowledge is trapped: you can't find it, can't export it, can't organize it, can't clean it up, and can't reference it. The more you use AI, the worse this gets. Your best insights are scattered across platforms you don't control.

## Solution

DouXia embeds your AI platforms directly inside a native desktop app. You chat as usual — DouXia quietly captures, stores, and organizes every conversation on your own computer. Search across all platforms at once, tag and categorize with AI assistance, export to Markdown or PDF, and clean up hundreds of chats in seconds.

## Core Value

- **Never lose a conversation** — Auto-capture saves every chat in real time to your local SQLite database
- **Find anything instantly** — Full-text search (FTS5) across all your AI conversations, all platforms
- **Organize at scale** — AI-powered tagging, topic clustering, and knowledge graph visualization
- **Works offline** — All data lives on your computer — search, browse, and export without internet

## Features

### Embedded AI Chat + Auto-Capture

Use Doubao, Yuanbao, DeepSeek, or ChatGPT directly inside DouXia — the experience is identical to using them in your browser. Every conversation is automatically captured and stored locally.

### Unified Conversation Management

Browse, search, and filter all your AI conversations in one place — regardless of which platform they came from. Full-text search (FTS5) finds answers buried deep in long conversations.

### Smart Organization

AI auto-tagging, topic clustering, knowledge graph visualization, and custom tag management help you organize conversations at scale.

### AI-Powered Knowledge Extraction

Turn fragmented Q&A sessions into structured knowledge with AI summaries, knowledge cards, and smart cleanup recommendations.

### Flexible Export & Sharing

One-click export to Markdown, HTML, or PDF, plus LAN sharing so other devices on your local network can browse your knowledge base via browser.

### Multi-Platform Support

Capture and permanent delete for Doubao, Yuanbao, DeepSeek, and ChatGPT.

## Quick Start

1. Download the installer from GitHub Releases
2. Run the installer and follow the prompts
3. Launch DouXia
4. Choose your preferred AI platform and log in
5. Start chatting — conversations are auto-captured by default
6. Use the Knowledge tab to search, filter, and organize

## Privacy

Your data stays on your device. All conversations are stored locally in SQLite — nothing is uploaded to any server, ever. No account is required. Smart features (tagging, summaries, clustering) use your own API key, so you decide which AI provider to use and when. Core features work without internet.
